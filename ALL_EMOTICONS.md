## How to Use

### Method 1: Type Shortcuts
Just type the shortcut in your message:
- `Hey everyone! :)` Ôćĺ Shows message with smile emoticon
- `I love this <3` Ôćĺ Shows message with heart emoticon
- `LOL XD` Ôćĺ Shows message with laughing emoticon

### Method 2: Use the Picker
1. Click the smile icon button in the toolbar
2. Browse all 93 emoticons
3. Click any emoticon to insert it
4. The emoticon will appear in your message box
